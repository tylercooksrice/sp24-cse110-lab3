Summary

In this first meeting on 4/14/2024 of the Coding Kittens, we introduced ourselves to each other to get to know each other more. With this first meeting, we were able to learn more about each other and our personalities and hobbies. We then voted on what our team name should be which as seen is the Coding Kittens. In addition, we chose our mascot to be a kitten and our team font of Jetbrains Mono.
